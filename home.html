Presque Vu roughly translates to “the feeling that something is on the tip of your tongue.” The brand itself explores this feeling by using clothing to express uncharted emotions and feelings. Nostalgia, melancholy, and emotional attachment are all touchstones that go into the story of each design. Designs that straddle the line between stunning and subtle. A reflection of those who like to keep to themselves. Presque Vu prioritizes the look and quality of each piece while promoting a fair price. Streetwear is the foundation of the brand and the team hopes to share our love for quality, wearable streetwear clothing with you all. Sign up for our mailing list or follow us on Instagram for upcoming details. Thank you for your interest.
